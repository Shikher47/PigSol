Our Story

The Dark Genesis of PigSol

It all began with an idea—a dark, profit-driven vision by Salil Bhonsle, a man who sees beyond conventional ethics. Inspired by the possibilities of genetic manipulation, he sought to breed pig beasts: creatures enhanced to grow faster, bigger, and smarter. These beings, designed for maximum profit, would serve as the centerpiece of PigSol's global domination in the food industry.

But Salil was not just interested in manipulating pig DNA; he wanted to leave his mark—literally. In a secret lab, hidden from the eyes of the public and regulatory bodies, Salil's most audacious plan came to life. He decided to use his own genetic material—his semen—to extract specific fatty genes that would be integrated into the DNA of his pig beasts. The goal was simple but twisted: create a breed of super pigs that would possess his genetic traits, fattening quickly and producing copious amounts of meat. The perfect combination of efficiency and greed.

This genetic fusion wasn't just a scientific endeavor; it was a dark obsession. Salil believed that his "genetic signature" would give the pig beasts a unique quality that no other competitor could replicate. His followers, the so-called "Acolytes of Genetic Purity," worked tirelessly under his command, ensuring that each pig beast was an emblem of his genetic prowess. The Behemoth Pigs, as they came to be known, were grotesque creatures bred for one purpose: to feed the insatiable hunger for profit.

These pig beasts were not just animals—they were monstrosities, engineered in a manner where natural compassion was replaced by a relentless drive to consume and grow. PigSol marketed these monstrosities under the guise of "sustainable innovation," fooling the masses into believing they were the next evolution in food supply, while in reality, they were the first step towards a dystopian future of genetic exploitation.
